Tsunami Warning Systems

Tsunami warning devices are critical for detecting and alerting coastal communities about potential tsunamis. They use seismometers, DART buoys, and tide gauges to monitor underwater earthquakes, ocean pressure changes, and rising sea levels. Once a threat is detected, warnings are sent via sirens, radio, and mobile alerts to enable timely evacuations. These systems help reduce casualties and property damage by providing early warnings before tsunami waves reach the shore.
