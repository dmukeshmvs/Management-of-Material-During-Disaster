Flood Warning Systems

A Flood Warning System (FWS) is designed to detect, predict, and alert communities about potential flooding. Modern systems use IoT, AI, and GIS for real-time monitoring and accurate forecasting. By providing early warnings, these systems help reduce loss of life, property damage, and economic impact, ensuring better preparedness and response to floods. It consists of data collection through sensors and weather stations, analysis using hydrological models, and dissemination of warnings via SMS, sirens, and media.
