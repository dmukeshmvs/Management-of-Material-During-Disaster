About Our Team

Our Mission

We are a dedicated team of disaster management professionals committed to saving lives and reducing the impact of natural disasters through innovative technology and community engagement.

Our platform integrates real-time data analysis, risk assessment tools, and emergency response coordination to provide comprehensive disaster management solutions for governments, organizations, and individuals.

With expertise in meteorology, civil engineering, data science, and emergency response, we've developed a system that helps communities prepare for, respond to, and recover from natural disasters more effectively.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our services and may include:

  • Personal information you provide (name, email, contact details)
  • Location data for disaster alerts and response coordination
  • Usage data and preferences
  • Device information for system optimization

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Deliver personalized disaster alerts and safety information
  • Respond to emergencies and coordinate relief efforts
  • Conduct research to improve disaster preparedness
  • Ensure the security of our systems

Information Sharing

We may share non-personally identifiable information publicly and with partners to improve disaster response. Personal information is only shared:

  • With emergency services during disasters
  • When we have your consent
  • For legal reasons or to prevent harm

Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through our services or by other means.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your disaster predictions?

Our prediction models combine data from multiple reliable sources including meteorological agencies, geological surveys, and historical patterns. While no prediction system is 100% accurate, our algorithms achieve over 92% accuracy for major disaster events with at least 24 hours notice.

How can I receive alerts for my area?

You can register on our platform and enable location services to receive customized alerts via SMS, email, or our mobile app. We also provide options for community leaders to receive alerts for multiple locations.

What should I include in my emergency preparedness kit?

A basic emergency kit should include: water (1 gallon per person per day), non-perishable food, flashlight with extra batteries, first aid kit, medications, multi-tool, sanitation supplies, copies of important documents, cash, emergency blanket, and local maps. We provide customized kit recommendations based on your location and family needs.

How can my community partner with your organization?

We offer several partnership programs including community training sessions, infrastructure assessment, and customized emergency response planning. Contact our outreach team through the website to discuss options tailored to your community's needs and risks.

Do you provide services internationally?

Yes, we work with international agencies and local governments worldwide. Our technology is adaptable to different regions and disaster types. Currently, we have active monitoring in 14 countries across multiple continents.

How is your service funded?

We operate through a combination of government grants, corporate sponsorships, and nonprofit funding. Basic alert services are free to individuals, while advanced features and organizational services may require subscription or partnership agreements.
