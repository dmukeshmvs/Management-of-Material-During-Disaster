Additional Tips for Emergency Preparedness

Being prepared goes beyond having an emergency kit. Here are some additional tips to ensure your safety:

  • Create an emergency plan with your family.
  • Practice drills for different types of emergencies.
  • Stay informed about local hazards and alerts.
  • Keep important documents in a safe and accessible place.
  • Establish a communication plan with family members.
