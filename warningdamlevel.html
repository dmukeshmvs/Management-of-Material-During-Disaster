Dam Level Warning System

A Dam Level Warning System monitors water levels in dams to prevent flooding and structural failures. It uses sensors, weather data, and automated alerts to detect rising water levels and issue warnings through sirens, SMS, and mobile alerts. This system helps authorities manage controlled water releases and emergency responses, ensuring the safety of nearby communities and infrastructure.
