This system helps track the receipt, availability, movement, and distribution of materials during disasters.

Disasters, whether natural or man-made, cause widespread devastation, leaving communities in urgent need of essential supplies.
Proper management of materials during such crises plays a vital role in ensuring swift relief and recovery.
This process involves the receipt, availability, movement, and final distribution of resources to those in need.
